Upcycle, Re-purpose, & Reuse Materials

Savvy Creations stocks unique and hard-to-find re-purposing materials to reuse in your next upcycle project. We search the secondary market for architectural salvage, vintage advertisement, antique glass or farm salvage. Some of our current re-purpose material include:

  • Antique wood, metal & glass knobs
  • Advertising Crates
  • Vintage License Plates
  • Blue Glass Mason Jars
  • Advertising Milk Bottles
  • Assorted Antique Glass Bottles
  • Mid-century Maps of the Pittsburgh Area
  • Grain Sieve
  • Barn Hooks
  • Old locks and keys

** We are continuing to change our stock. To get the most accurate list of our upcycle material, please contact us.
